## What ApplyPilot Is

ApplyPilot is a cloud-based AI platform designed specifically for college application consulting agencies. It handles the repetitive, time-consuming parts of your workflow automatically — while keeping your coordinator in full control of every decision that matters.

Think of it as giving every coordinator a team of AI assistants that work around the clock, never make a typo, never forget a deadline, and ask for approval before taking any action that cannot be undone.

## What It Does

### Common App — Filled Automatically

ApplyPilot logs into Common App using the student's credentials and fills every section — Personal Information, Family, Education, Test Scores, Activities, Writing, Recommendations, and Payment — directly from the student profile your coordinator builds during intake.

After each section is filled, your coordinator receives a screenshot via Telegram and approves before the agent continues. Nothing is submitted without a final explicit approval from your team.

The result: what currently takes a coordinator 3–4 hours per student takes 20 minutes of your coordinator's attention and runs automatically in between.

### Essays and Recommendation Letters — Written in Seconds

The writing agent reads the student's full profile and generates:
- Personal statements tailored to the student's background and story
- Supplemental essays matching each university's specific prompts and word limits
- Recommendation letters written in the teacher's voice, based on their subject and relationship

Every piece of writing is scored for quality, checked against word limits, and sent to your coordinator for review and approval before it is used anywhere.

Your coordinator edits, approves, and moves on. No blank page. No starting from scratch.

### Deadlines — Never Missed

The platform tracks every application deadline for every student across every university. Your team receives automated Telegram alerts at 30 days, 14 days, 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before each deadline.

If a deadline is tomorrow and the task is not yet complete, the system sends emergency alerts at 7AM, 12PM, and 6PM until it is resolved.

No spreadsheet to maintain. No deadline slips through.

### Student Emails — Monitored and Drafted

The platform monitors student email accounts hourly and automatically classifies every incoming email — university updates, document requests, interview invitations, and decisions. When an acceptance, rejection, or waitlist decision arrives, your coordinator receives an instant notification.

For emails that require a response, the platform drafts a reply in your coordinator's voice. The draft is sent for approval before anything is sent.

### Documents — Organized Automatically

When a student uploads a transcript, passport, or test score report, the platform reads the document using AI, categorizes it correctly, and updates the checklist automatically. Your coordinator sees at a glance exactly which documents are received and which are still missing — for every student, updated in real time.

## The Approval System

ApplyPilot operates on a simple principle: the AI does the work, the human makes the decisions.

Every action falls into one of three categories:

**Automatic** — document processing, email reading, deadline tracking, daily briefings. No action required from your team.

**Approval required** — essays, rec letters, email replies, Common App section fills. The AI prepares the output. Your coordinator reviews and approves. Only then does it proceed.

**Hard gate** — application submission, sending emails from student accounts. A full stop. Your coordinator must give explicit approval. There is no way for the system to proceed without it.

This means your coordinator is never out of the loop on anything that matters — but they are freed from everything that doesn't require their judgment.

## Security and Privacy

Student data is handled with the same standards as financial and medical information.

Every agency's data is completely isolated from every other agency. It is technically impossible for one agency to see another's students, documents, or credentials — this is enforced at the database level, not just in the application.

Student email passwords and portal credentials are encrypted using AES-256 military-grade encryption before storage. They are decrypted only in memory during active tasks and are never logged, never written to files, and never visible to anyone except your designated admin.

Every action taken by any agent or any team member is logged with a timestamp, creating a complete audit trail for every student.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the student need to know ApplyPilot is being used?**
That is entirely your decision. The platform acts on behalf of your coordinator — it uses credentials and writes in voices your coordinator controls. How you communicate your process to students is your business, not ours.

**What if Common App changes their layout?**
The browser agent is built to handle layout changes, and updates are deployed automatically. In rare cases where a specific section fails, your coordinator is notified immediately and can fill that section manually while we update the agent.

**Can two coordinators work on the same student?**
Yes. Multiple staff members can access any student's profile simultaneously. The audit log tracks exactly who did what and when.

**What happens to our data if we cancel?**
Your data remains in your account for 90 days after cancellation, giving you time to export everything. After that, it is permanently deleted.

**Is there a limit on document storage?**
No. Document storage is included at no additional cost.

**Can we use it for UK, European, or other non-US applications?**
Currently the platform is optimized for US applications via Common App. Support for UCAS (UK) and European university portals is on the roadmap. Email management, essay writing, deadline tracking, and document organization work for any country's applications today.
